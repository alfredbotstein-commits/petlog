# PetLog — Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 1, 2026
**Developer:** Loopspur LLC

## Overview

PetLog ("the App") is a pet care tracking application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

## Data Collection

**PetLog does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.**

All data you enter into PetLog — including pet profiles, activities, vet visits, reminders, and photos — is stored **locally on your device only** using IndexedDB (browser storage) or the device's local database.

### Data Stored Locally
- Pet profiles (name, species, breed, birthday, weight, photos)
- Care activities (feeding, walking, grooming logs)
- Vet visit records (clinic, vet, diagnoses, vaccinations)
- Reminders (medication, appointments)
- App preferences (theme, units, date format)

### Data NOT Collected
- No personal information (name, email, phone)
- No location data
- No device identifiers
- No usage analytics or tracking
- No advertising data
- No data shared with third parties

## In-App Purchases

PetLog offers a one-time Premium upgrade ($2.99) processed through the Google Play Store or Apple App Store. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by the respective platform. Loopspur does not receive or store any payment information.

## Data Export

You may export your data at any time via Settings → Export All Data (JSON). This creates a local file on your device that you control.

## Data Deletion

You may delete individual pets (and all associated data) from within the app, or uninstall the app to remove all data. Since no data is stored externally, uninstalling the app permanently deletes all PetLog data.

## Children's Privacy

PetLog does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The App stores no personal data of any user.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
